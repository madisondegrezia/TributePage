Steve Jobs

The man who transformed the tech industry.

Monumental Parts of Steve Jobs' Life:

  • 1955: Born on February 24, 1955 in San Francisco, CA.
  • 1976: He co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak and introduced the Apple I computer.
  • 1984: Apple introduces the Macintosh.
  • 1985: Jobs leaves Apple because of disagreements with management.
  • 1985:After leaving Apple, he launches a computer company called NeXT.
  • 1996: Rejoins Apple as a consultant.
  • 2000: Jobs becomes the permanent CEO of Apple.
  • 2001: Apple introduces their creation of a portable mp3 player, called an iPod.
  • 2003: Diagnosed with pancreatic cancer.
  • 2011: Jobs resigns as CEO of Apple.
  • 2011: Jobs unfortunately passes at the age of 56 on October 5th, 2011.
